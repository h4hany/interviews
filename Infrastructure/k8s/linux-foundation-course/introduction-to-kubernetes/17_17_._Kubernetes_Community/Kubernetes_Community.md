# Kubernetes Community

With over [106K GitHub stars](https://github.com/kubernetes/kubernetes/), Kubernetes is one of the most popular open source projects. The community members not only help with the source code, but they also help with sharing the knowledge. The community engages in both online and offline activities.

Most valuable contributors to the Kubernetes project are recognized on a yearly basis and are presented with a Contributor Award. A list of the award recipients, sorted by year, can be found [here](https://www.kubernetes.dev/community/awards/). Their contribution can be in the form of code, documentation, attending and speaking at meetups, answering questions on forums, etc.
